Why N Portas

Why N Portas?
N Portas means N Doors in Portuguese. N is the mathematical variable that can be any given number N.
As students, we spend a lot of time digressing from the main purpose in life. How many of you have been reproached for hours by your parents, when you were young, about your job being going to school and study and about getting to be someone in life? How many of you have heard the famous sentence “you have to do more”, knowing not the meaning of the latter? I bet I wasn’t the only one. My course has been similar to that of many: full of uncertainties; disappointment; struggle. There has been a time in which I wanted to give up on studying and simply get into the job market to acquire experience, for that’s what really counts.
Or is it?
Well, after I have enrolled in University, I have started to understand how vast the academy universe is and how many Doors would I be able to open by means of knowledge. N Doors.
A world full of people whose richness in knowledge and different perspectives unite in a world of communication. Debates, discussions, exchanging ideas, talks, exhibitions, conferences. Each sentence carries a meaning and every word weighs. It’s on us, as listeners and as students of the world to evaluate each word’s weight in the sentence.
Do you want to go inside the knowledge room and find out how many doors can you open?

Work


Tutorial Sessions

Feel like something’s missing, but you don’t know what it is? Have a presentation to make, but need help structuring your train of thought? We can work on it together. In the tutorial sessions we’ll better your projects in detail by means of suggestions and comments. Whatever the phase you’re in I’ll meet you.

What you need to know about the tutorial session.

The tutorial sessions are an hour sessions in which we work and prepare our studies together. As we want everything to go according to plan and save time, the first session will have the duration of about one hour and fifteen minutes to thirty minutes so we can get to know the working method we are to adopt and to talk a bit about what have got you to ask for this kind of support.
There are good news, though! The first fifteen to thirty minutes are offered to all the new students.
Are you ready to learn your way?

Proofreading

Do you need to submit a written work, but you are so tired you cannot read it properly?
Do you have so much going on in your life you do not even have time to read what you have written?
Leave it to me. I can proofread your work altogether and in no time you can have it back with suggestions and comments so you can have it grammatically correct and clear and modify it the way you see fit.

About Me

During my course I have found out that the best way to improve oneself is through sharing knowledge and experiences. My main goal is to provide support during students’ apprenticeship and their academic course, and make professionals’ work simpler and clearer.

And now, about myself:
Bachelor’s degree in Linguistics and Master’s student in Comparative Studies. Book-eater and knowledge lover. Non-published writer and tutor. Looking for ever improving myself. The best is on education both on children and adults who haven’t gotten the opportunity to access education sooner; that’s why I have taken the challenge of teaching Creative Writer at the Senior University and Experimental Sciences to kids at elementary schools.
I feel ready to improve myself helping others as best I can. Let’s work together, grow stronger and wiser, and build a brighter future, shall we?
